LARGE HADRON COLLIDER AND ANTIMATTER

Conclusion

As a result of the research, the structure of the Large Hadron Collider, its purpose and goals were examined, and the research results were thoroughly investigated. A particle of the Standard Model was constructed and dissected.

By analyzing the scientific topic, we understood how the Hadron Collider was built, how the discovery of antimatter, bosons, and leptons helped expand the standard model. Furthermore, if there are more studies at CERN, I believe they will focus on researching dark matter, a possible "gravitron." In my opinion, this could be the subject of my next research work.

In conclusion, the Hadron Collider is the key to understanding the universe. And everything that has been discovered and explored is only a small part of what still needs to be investigated.
